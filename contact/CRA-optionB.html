Tax debt refers to any taxes that you owe to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) after the filing deadline. The CRA cannot simply cancel tax debt, so you must file an objection if you believe you should not owe that debt.

Penalties and interest refer to specific fees. The CRA does have some discretion to cancel or waive penalties or interest.
