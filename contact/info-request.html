Information request

You have the right to request additional information related to pesticide regulation from the Pest Management Regulatory Agency. Answering a few questions will help PMRA respond to your requests. Please note that the more specific your request, the easier it is to respond to.

Q1: Are you looking for information related to:

  • An open consultation

    Q2: Do you have the reference number for the document(s) you are interested in?

    • Yes
    • No

      Q3: What sort of information are you looking for?

      • Externally-submitted information (ex: study, applicant-submitted form)
        Alert: Info

        If the information you are requesting contains confidential test data, you will be required to submit an affidavit before accessing the material. Learn more about the inspection of confidential test data.

        Q4: Are you interested in information related to a specific evaluation stream?

        • Chemistry (not designated as Confidential Business Information)
        • Human Health (Toxicology, Food Residue, Occupational Exposure)
        • Environment (Environmental chemistry, Environmental Fate)
        • Efficacy (Crop Tolerance Data, Value)
        • I don't know
      • PMRA-generated documentation (ex: risk assessments, data evaluation records)
      • I don't know
  • Specific pesticide, pest, crop or organism

Result

Please direct your request to InfoServe at pmra.info-arla@hc-sc.gc.ca

Please include:

  • Your name and contact information
  • The name of the consultation prompting this request
  • Your interest in studies related to Human Health

Of note:

  • Requesting specific document(s) with their PMRA reference number may result in a quicker response.
  • Since you selected "externally-submitted information", you may be required to submit an affidavit before accessing the information. Learn more about the inspection of confidential test data.
