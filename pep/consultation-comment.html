Privacy notice statement

Collection and use of personal information in this application is in accordance with the federal Privacy Act. The personal information you provide is collected under the authority of section 4(2) of the Pest Control Products Act for the purposes of allowing the public to have the opportunity to participate in the pest control product regulatory process and for PMRA to communicate with you as part of this process. For more information refer to the Personal Information Bank at PSU 914 - Public Communications at infosource.gc.ca.

The Privacy Act. gives you the rights of access to, correction of and protection of your personal information. For further information about these rights, please contact Health Canada's Director - Privacy Management Division by phone at 613-948-1219 or by e-mail at hc.privacy-vie.privee.sc@canada.ca. You also have the right to file a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you think your personal information has been handled improperly.

Security notice: Up to Protected B only
