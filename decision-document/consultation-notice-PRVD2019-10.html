Consultation: Pyriproxyfen, Proposed Re-evaluation Decision PRVD2019-10

Current status: Open

Opened on March 21, 2022 and will close for consultation on May 20, 2022

Health Canada's Pest Management Regulatory Agency is seeking feedback on the Proposed Re-evaluation Decision PRVD2019-10, Pyriproxyfen and Its Associated End-use Products.

  • Pyriproxyfen is a pesticide product.
  • It was approved by Health Canada in 1997.
  • This consultation is on a regular 15-year cyclical re-evaluation assessment.
  • Health Canada invites your feedback on the proposed re-evaluation decision.

What is the proposed decision

The results from the scientific re-evaluation shows that pyriproxyfen still meets Health Canada's requirements for the protection of human health, the environment, and it is needed and effective for use in Canada.

View a summary of the proposed decision

What is the focus of this consultation

This consultation is related to the following uses of pyriproxyfen:

  • Commercial use in greenhouses
  • Commercial indoor use to control fleas
  • Domestic use in your home or on pets

Join in: how to participate

Please refer to the PMRA Publications Section of our website for contact information and to learn about how we will use the information you provide to us.

Mention the document title (PMRA (PRVD2019-10) Pyriproxyfen and Its Associated End-use Products) in your communications with us.
