Pesticides and pest management consultations

The public is invited to review and respond to proposed major registration decisions, including new registrations, major new users of a pesticide, re-evaluations and special reviews. The following table includes information about all proposals that are currently open for consultation.

You can read the consultation notice by selecting the link associated with the ID of the proposal (for example, PRD2022-XX for a proposed registration decision posted in 2022). You can also get more information about the active ingredient under review by clicking the link associated with the name of the active ingredient.
