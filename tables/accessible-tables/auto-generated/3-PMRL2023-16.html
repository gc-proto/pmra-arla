International situation and trade implications
To mitigate human health risks associated with the dietary exposure to piperonyl butoxide, an
MRL is proposed for revocation. Table 2 compares the MRL proposed for revocation in Canada
for piperonyl butoxide with corresponding American tolerances and Codex MRLs.
American tolerances are listed in the Electronic Code of Federal Regulations, 40 CFR Part 180,
by pesticide, and a listing of established Codex MRLs
